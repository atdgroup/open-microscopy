Shutter



Open- Opens the shutter.
Close -Closes the shutter.
Shutter Open - Light is on when shutter is open..
PC control - ON - PC controls; Off - Manual control through Lamp Shutter buttons on control box.
Inhibit -  In inhibit mode the shutter does not actually open but just sends a signal to the camera to acquire an image. Inhibit is on when the bright filed modality is chosen (Microscope >> mode >> Bright field)
Close - Closes the dialog box.
